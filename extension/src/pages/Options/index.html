INSTRUCTIONSCRAFTER·ABOUT

About this extension.

InstructionsCrafter turns a screen recording or a series of clicks into Microsoft Word step-by-step instructions.

The screen and audio capture is built on a modified version of Screenity by Alyssa X, released under the GNU General Public License v3.
